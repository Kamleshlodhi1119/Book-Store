Return Policy

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your book, you can return it within 60 days of purchase.

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you that we have received your returned item. We will initiate a refund to your original method of payment immediately.

Shipping Policy

Standard shipping usually takes 3-5 business days. International shipping times may vary based on location.
